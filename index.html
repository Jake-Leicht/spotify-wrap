Want to checkout your Spotify Wrap?

  • 1. Go to Spotify For Developers and log in using your Spotify account

  • 2. Next go to your dashboard (located in the top right corner). Click your name then dashboard

  • 3. (Create your app) click the 'create app' button

    Most of the inputs are optional, just give your app a name (like Spotify Wrap) and in the 'Redirect URI' input, enter " http://localhost:5173/callback ". Check the terms and services and click "save". (If it asks you to enter a description, fill it in with any optional text: such as 'Spotify Wrap')

  • 4. Finally, click on your created app and go to Settings. You'll find it in the top right corner of the 'Home' page for your app

  • 5. Copy your Client ID and paste it into the input bar below

  • 6. Finally, click submit!

    *Note: none of your personal or Spotify information is being stored or collected. To assure safety concerns, afterwards you can delete the app you created on the Spotify Developers Website preventing any access to your Spotify info, by deleting the Client Id used to access it.*
